# Madrasa Career Counselling & Educational Resources

## Introduction

This project provides open educational resources for career counselling, academic guidance, and employability support for madrasa students and graduates.

Many madrasa students have strong religious, linguistic, ethical, and intellectual training. However, they often need structured guidance regarding higher education pathways, career planning, skill development, and employment opportunities.

The purpose of this repository is to provide practical templates, guidance materials, and session resources for teachers, counsellors, administrators, and students working in madrasa education systems.

## Objectives

- To support career counselling sessions for madrasa students.
- To provide templates for student guidance and academic planning.
- To develop simple resources for employability awareness.
- To connect traditional Islamic education with contemporary academic and professional pathways.
- To assist counsellors in planning, conducting, and documenting guidance sessions.

## Contents

This repository may include:

- Career counselling session outlines
- Student information forms
- Guidance templates
- Employability skill checklists
- Academic pathway resources
- Urdu-English educational support materials
- Research and curriculum planning templates

## Author

This project is maintained by an academic and educational counsellor working in Arabic, Islamic Studies, education, curriculum development, and career counselling.
